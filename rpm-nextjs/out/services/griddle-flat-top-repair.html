Frequently Asked Questions

Why does my griddle have hot and cold spots?

Uneven heating usually indicates burner issues, surface warping, or thermostat problems. We diagnose the cause and restore even heat distribution.

How often should commercial griddles be serviced?

We recommend annual professional service including thermostat calibration, burner cleaning, and safety inspection. High-volume operations may benefit from semi-annual service.

Can warped griddle surfaces be repaired?

Minor warping can sometimes be addressed, but severe warping typically requires surface replacement. We'll assess and provide honest recommendations.

Do you service chrome-top griddles?

Yes, we service all griddle types including chrome-top, steel, and composite surface griddles from all major manufacturers.
