Common Issues We Fix

Oven Not Heating

Faulty igniter, burner issues, or heating element failure. We diagnose and repair quickly.

Uneven Cooking

Convection fan problems, temperature sensor issues, or calibration needed.

Gas Smell

Gas leak detection and repair. Safety is our priority.

Temperature Fluctuations

Thermostat calibration, sensor replacement, or control board repair.

Door Not Sealing

Gasket replacement to maintain proper heat retention.

Pilot Light Issues

Pilot assembly cleaning, thermocouple replacement.

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you repair my commercial oven?

We offer same-day service for most commercial oven repairs in the Memphis area. Call before noon and we'll typically have a technician to you the same day.

Do you repair all brands of commercial ovens?

Yes, our technicians are trained on all major commercial oven brands including Vulcan, Garland, Blodgett, Bakers Pride, Southbend, Imperial, and many more.

What does commercial oven repair cost?

Repair costs vary depending on the issue. We provide upfront pricing after diagnosis. Most repairs range from $150-$500 for parts and labor.

Should I repair or replace my commercial oven?

Generally, if repair costs exceed 50% of replacement cost, or if your oven is over 15 years old with frequent issues, replacement may be more economical. We'll give you honest advice.
