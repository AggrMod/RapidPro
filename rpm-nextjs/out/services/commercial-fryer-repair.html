Common Issues We Fix

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should commercial fryers be serviced?

We recommend professional maintenance every 6 months for high-volume operations, annually for moderate use. Regular service extends equipment life and maintains efficiency.

Why is my fryer taking longer to heat up?

Slow heating usually indicates heating element degradation, scale buildup, or gas pressure issues. Our technicians can diagnose and resolve the problem quickly.

Can you repair pressure fryers?

Yes, we service all types of commercial fryers including pressure fryers from Henny Penny, BKI, and others. Pressure fryers require specialized knowledge we have.

What brands of commercial fryers do you repair?

We repair all major brands including Pitco, Frymaster, Henny Penny, Vulcan, Imperial, Dean, and many more.
