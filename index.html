Beverage Station & Slushy Machine Q&A

Quick checks your team can run before placing a service call, plus signs it is time for onsite repair.

Beverage Station

  • Q: Drinks are dispensing slowly.
    A: Check syrup bag level, CO2 pressure, and clogged nozzles first.
  • Q: Unit leaks under the counter.
    A: Inspect quick connects and drain lines for loose fittings or cracked seals.
  • Q: Flavor tastes off.
    A: Run a full line sanitization cycle and replace overdue water filters.

Slushy Machine

  • Q: Mix stays liquid and will not freeze.
    A: Verify freeze mode, sugar ratio, and condenser airflow.
  • Q: Barrel motor stops turning.
    A: Check breaker status and drive coupling wear before restarting.
  • Q: Machine trips the breaker.
    A: Confirm a dedicated commercial circuit and call for an amp-draw test.
