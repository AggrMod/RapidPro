Refrigeration Quick Diagnostics

Quick checks your team can run before placing a service call, plus signs it is time for onsite repair.

Read Full Refrigeration Troubleshooting Guide

Reach-In Cooler

  • Q: Not holding temperature.
    A: Check door gasket seal, condenser coil for dust buildup, and thermostat setting first.
  • Q: Frost buildup on evaporator.
    A: Inspect defrost timer and evaporator fan — blocked airflow is the usual cause.
  • Q: Unit runs constantly but stays warm.
    A: Low refrigerant or a dirty condenser — call for a service visit.

Walk-In Cooler

  • Q: Temperature fluctuating.
    A: Inspect door gasket and strip curtains for gaps — cold air escaping is the most common cause.
  • Q: Ice forming on ceiling or walls.
    A: Check drain line for blockages and verify evaporator fan is running.
  • Q: Door not sealing properly.
    A: Gasket replacement or hinge adjustment — we carry common sizes for same-day repair.

Failed a Health Inspection?

Cold-holding violations are the #1 reason Memphis restaurants get flagged. Most are caused by worn gaskets, sagging doors, or blocked drain lines — not mechanical failures. We fix the hardware so you pass re-inspection.
