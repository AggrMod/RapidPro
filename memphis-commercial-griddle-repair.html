Frequently Asked Questions

How much does commercial griddle repair cost in Memphis?

Commercial griddle repair typically costs $100-$350. Thermostat replacement runs $125-$200, heating element or burner repair $150-$300. Call us at (901) 257-9417 for a free estimate.

Why is my griddle not heating evenly?

Uneven heating is usually caused by a warped surface, malfunctioning thermostat, clogged burners, or failed heating elements. Our technicians can diagnose whether you need calibration or part replacement.

How often should commercial griddles be serviced?

Every 6 months for most restaurants. High-volume breakfast spots may need quarterly maintenance. Regular service ensures even heating and prevents costly breakdowns.

What griddle brands do you repair?

We repair Vulcan, Garland, Star Manufacturing, APW Wyott, Toastmaster, Keating, and more. Flat tops, chrome griddles, grooved units - we service them all.

Why does my griddle have hot and cold spots?

Hot and cold spots indicate clogged burners, failed heating zones, a warped plate, or thermostat issues. Call (901) 257-9417 for same-day diagnosis and repair.
