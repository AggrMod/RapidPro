Frequently Asked Questions

How much does commercial fryer repair cost in Memphis?

Commercial fryer repair typically costs $125-$400 depending on the issue. Thermostat replacement runs $150-$250, heating elements $175-$350. Call us at (901) 257-9417 for a free estimate with no hidden fees.

Why is my commercial fryer not heating up?

Usually caused by a faulty thermostat, burned out heating element, tripped high-limit switch, or power issue. For gas fryers, check the pilot light and thermocouple. Our technicians can diagnose and repair same-day.

How often should commercial fryers be serviced?

Every 3-6 months depending on usage. High-volume restaurants need quarterly maintenance. Regular service extends fryer life by 40% and prevents costly emergency repairs.

What fryer brands do you repair?

We repair Pitco, Frymaster, Henny Penny, Vulcan, Imperial, Dean, Anets, and more. Deep fryers, pressure fryers, countertop units - we service them all throughout Memphis.

Why is my fryer oil foaming or smoking?

Foaming indicates degraded oil or moisture contamination. Smoking means overheating - likely a faulty thermostat. Replace the oil first. If smoking continues, call (901) 257-9417 for thermostat calibration.
