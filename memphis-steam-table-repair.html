Frequently Asked Questions

How much does steam table repair cost in Memphis?

Steam table repair typically costs $100-$300. Heating element replacement runs $125-$225, thermostat repair $100-$175. Call us at (901) 257-9417 for a free estimate.

Why is my steam table not heating?

Usually caused by a failed heating element, tripped high-limit switch, faulty thermostat, or low water level. Check the water first - most units won't heat without adequate water.

How often should steam tables be serviced?

Every 6-12 months for most operations. Buffet restaurants need more frequent maintenance. Regular service ensures food safety temperatures and prevents breakdowns.

What steam table brands do you repair?

We repair Wells, Vollrath, Duke, Hatco, Eagle, Delfield, Alto-Shaam, and more. Wet and dry steam tables, drop-in wells, and portable units throughout Memphis.

Why does my steam table have uneven heating?

Uneven heating indicates failed individual elements, clogged circulation, mineral buildup, or thermostat issues. We can repair individual wells without replacing the whole unit.
